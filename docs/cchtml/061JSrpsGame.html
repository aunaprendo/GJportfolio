Let's play Squirrel, Nut, Tree!

Rules to the game

You will be playing against the computer.

You can choose between Squirrel, Nut, and Tree.

The first one to three points wins.

Here are the rules to getting a point in the game:

  • Squirrel beats Tree
  • Tree beats Nut
  • Nut beats Squirrel

If the player and computer choose the same option (e.g., Nut and Nut), then no one gets the point.
